Brindex.co.ke is an online marketing website that leverage on the progressive growth in the higher learning institution to facilitate the uptake of course in the institution. Brindex.co.ke is an online mall of higher learning institution. The process of selecting a more affordable course, from an institution of the students choice, for the optimal duration preferred by the student in question. The exam body that is examining each and every course will be cleary indicated on the search results.Marketing on the website will enable the firms to enjoy fairness in marketing. The cost of marketing will considerably reduced since the firms will be advertised collectively.

The diagrams below indicate the process through which the student is expected to undertake to get the course of his choice.
